Tour Overview

Experience the magic of the Sahara Desert
with our premium 3-day expedition. This unforgettable journey takes you from the bustling streets of Marrakech through the majestic Atlas Mountains and into the golden dunes of the Sahara.

Travel in comfort aboard our air-conditioned 4x4 vehicles as you traverse diverse landscapes. Visit the UNESCO World Heritage site of Aït Benhaddou, ride camels through the desert at sunset, and spend a night under the stars in our luxury desert camp.

Tour Highlights

Atlas Mountains

Cross the stunning High Atlas Mountains through the Tizi n'Tichka pass with panoramic views.

Aït Benhaddou

Explore this ancient fortified village and UNESCO World Heritage site with a local guide.

Desert Camp

Overnight stay in a luxury desert camp with proper beds, private bathrooms, and authentic cuisine.

Berber Experience

Enjoy traditional Berber music, storytelling, and stargazing around the campfire.

Camel Trek

Experience a camel ride through the golden dunes at sunset and sunrise.

Desert Sunrise

Wake up early to witness the magical sunrise over the Sahara dunes.
